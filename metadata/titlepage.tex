\noindent
\bfseries
Abstract.
\normalfont{}
In this work, we propose a topological field theory that generates the Lovelock-Cartan family of gravity theories. This is accomplished via to the existence of a topological symmetry that, when explictly broken, releases the degrees of freedom of gravity. Additionally, we prove the quantum consistency of the model, to all orders in perturbation theory, using the algebraic renormalization technique.
